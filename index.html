About Hon. Kevin Ogoli

Hon. Kevin Ogoli is a dedicated medical practitioner and aspiring political leader who has committed his life to serving the community of Kiptuiya Ward. With a strong background in healthcare and a deep understanding of community needs, he is preparing to vie for the position of Member of County Assembly in 2027.

Currently serving as a medical practitioner at both Kapsabet District Hospital and Eldoret Referral Hospital, Hon. Ogoli brings a wealth of experience in public health and community service to his leadership role.

Professional Background

  • Medical Practitioner at Kapsabet District Hospital
  • Specialist at Eldoret Referral Hospital
  • Community Health Advocate
  • Aspiring MCA Candidate 2027

His unique combination of medical expertise and community leadership makes him well-positioned to understand and address the healthcare and development needs of Kiptuiya Ward. Hon. Ogoli believes in a holistic approach to community development, where health, education, and infrastructure work together to create sustainable progress.
